About William Hung

William Hung is a Data Management & Analytics professional and transformational speaker who discovered his true calling through an extraordinary journey of reinvention. From his viral American Idol moment to his #1 independent album "Inspiration" in 2004, to his current role specializing in complex research and analytics for Los Angeles County's Aging & Disabilities Department, William brings a unique perspective on resilience, transformation, and finding purpose in service to others.

Currently serving 9.7+ million county residents, William transforms multifaceted data from federal, state, and local sources into actionable insights that drive strategic decision-making and support vulnerable populations. His expertise spans complex data analysis, multi-platform dashboard development, and regulatory compliance with federal Older Americans Act requirements.

With a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and an MBA from Marist College, plus extensive experience in data science workflows and systematic decision-making frameworks, William combines technical expertise with personal transformation experience to deliver powerful, practical insights on resilience, career reinvention, and finding meaning in public service.
